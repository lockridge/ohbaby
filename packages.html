About

Oh Baby! is a family-owned and operated 3D/4D ultrasound center and baby boutique located in Campbellsville, Kentucky. We specialize in elective, non-diagnostic pregnancy ultrasounds using brand new, state-of-the art equipment in a clean, private and comfortable setting. All of our ultrasounds are performed by an ARDMS-registered sonographer. We also carry many products for your new bundle of love, such as Aden+Anais™, Wubbanubs™, Bottle Pets™ and much more. We are honored that you've chosen Oh Baby! to help capture these lasting memories for your family
